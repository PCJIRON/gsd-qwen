# Questioning Framework

## Purpose
Extract complete project requirements through structured questioning.

---

## Question Categories

### 1. Problem Discovery

**Goal:** Understand the problem deeply

**Questions:**
- What problem are you trying to solve?
- Who has this problem?
- How do they solve it today?
- What's wrong with current solutions?
- How often does this problem occur?
- What happens if this problem isn't solved?

---

### 2. User Discovery

**Goal:** Understand target users

**Questions:**
- Who are your primary users?
- What are their goals?
- What are their pain points?
- What's their technical level?
- How will they discover your product?
- What motivates them to use it?

---

### 3. Solution Discovery

**Goal:** Understand the proposed solution

**Questions:**
- What does your solution do?
- How does it solve the problem?
- What's the core innovation?
- What are the key features?
- How is it different from existing solutions?
- What's your unique value proposition?

---

### 4. Technical Discovery

**Goal:** Understand technical requirements

**Questions:**
- What type of application is this?
- What platforms must it support?
- What's your preferred tech stack?
- Are there any technical constraints?
- What's your deployment strategy?
- What are your performance requirements?
- What are your security requirements?

---

### 5. Scope Discovery

**Goal:** Define project boundaries

**Questions:**
- What's your timeline?
- What's your budget (if applicable)?
- What's must-have vs nice-to-have?
- What's explicitly out of scope?
- What are your success criteria?
- How will you measure success?

---

### 6. Risk Discovery

**Goal:** Identify potential risks

**Questions:**
- What are the biggest technical risks?
- What are the biggest business risks?
- What dependencies do you have?
- What could cause this project to fail?
- How can you mitigate these risks?

---

## Question Flow

```
1. Problem Discovery (5 min)
   ↓
2. User Discovery (5 min)
   ↓
3. Solution Discovery (10 min)
   ↓
4. Technical Discovery (10 min)
   ↓
5. Scope Discovery (5 min)
   ↓
6. Risk Discovery (5 min)
```

---

## Tips

### Do's
- Ask open-ended questions
- Listen actively
- Follow up on interesting points
- Clarify ambiguities
- Document answers verbatim

### Don'ts
- Don't lead the witness
- Don't assume technical knowledge
- Don't skip difficult questions
- Don't rush
- Don't judge ideas

---

## Red Flags

🚩 **Watch for:**
- Vague problem statements
- Undefined target users
- "Everyone" as users
- No differentiation from existing solutions
- Unrealistic timelines
- No success metrics

---

## Output

After questioning, create:
1. PROJECT.md — Project overview
2. REQUIREMENTS.md — Detailed requirements
3. ROADMAP.md — Implementation phases
